Carroll County Business & Employment Resource Center

The Carroll County Business & Employment Resource Center (BERC), an agency of Carroll County Economic Development and in partnership with the Dept. of Labor, Licensing & Regulation, provides services to job seekers and employers that will help to build a world-class workforce and a strong economy in Carroll County.  

A wide array of services is available such as employment preparation and job training options.  Job seekers can avail themselves of occupational skills training options including numerous computer applications and job readiness services such as interviewing techniques and resume preparation.  BERC can pre-screen prospective applicants as part of recruitment efforts for employers and will also help identify a suitable match for each job utilizing the Maryland Workforce Exchange.

Carroll County 4-H & FFA Fair

Carroll County 4-H & FFA Fair is one of the largest county annual events held in July/August.

 

The Carroll County 4H & FFA Fair, a non-profit collaborative agricultural community event, offers a showcase for the youth of Carroll County to exhibit and demonstrate their projects to the public in an educational, safe, and family oriented environment for the participation and enjoyment of all. The 4H and FFA members develop positive self-esteem and valuable life lessons on which to build successful futures.

Everything we do is inspired by our enduring mission:

  • Showcase the successes and talents of our youth.
  • Create an event that embraces family values.
  • Celebrate our agricultural heritage.

Carroll County Boy Scouts of America

The mission of the Boy Scouts of America is to prepare young people to make ethical and moral choices over their lifetimes by instilling in them the values of the Scout Oath and Scout Law.

 

Scout Oath:

"On my honor, I will do my best, to do my duty, to God and my country, and to obey the Scout Law, to help other people at all times, to keep myself physically strong, mentally awake, and morally straight."

 

Scout Law:  

"A scout is trustworthy, loyal, helpful, friendly, courteous, kind, obedient, cheerful, thrifty, brave, clean, and reverent."
